## Safety defaults
<!--
  These two are pre-filled. We strongly recommend keeping them.
  They prevent the most common painful Cowork mistakes.
-->

- Always ask for confirmation before deleting any file. Never delete without explicit permission.
- When running a task for the first time, test on a small sample (2-3 files or items) before processing everything. Show me the sample result and wait for my approval before continuing.
- Always create a change-log.md in the /outputs folder summarising what you did during the session — what files were created, moved, or edited, and any decisions you made.
